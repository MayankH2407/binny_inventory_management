Install on Your Phone  Recommended

Install the app on your home screen for a native app-like experience — full-screen mode, QR scanner, and offline support.

Open in Chrome

Open the app link above in Google Chrome browser.

Tap the Install Banner

You should see an "Install Binny Inventory" banner at the bottom. Tap Install.

Or Use the Menu

If no banner appears: tap the three-dot menu ⋮ in Chrome, then select "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".

Done!

The Binny Inventory icon will appear on your home screen. Open it like any other app.

Open in Safari

Open the app link above in Safari (not Chrome — iOS requires Safari for PWA install).

Tap the Share Button

Tap the Share icon ↑ (square with upward arrow) at the bottom of the screen.

Add to Home Screen

Scroll down in the share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen", then tap Add.

Done!

The Binny Inventory icon will appear on your home screen.
